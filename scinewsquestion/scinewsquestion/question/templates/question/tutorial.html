Instruction (1/3)

In this HIT, you are going to read 3 news articles on ONE scientific research. Each article tries to deliver the same scientific research findings to their audiences.

Your goal is: after reading each article, finding out what valuable information each article does not contain and contains based on your understanding.

To be specific, you are going to finish two tasks:

  1. You will read an article to find out what information each article does not contain. Based on what you find you need to generate a list of questions that each article cannot answer.
  2. You will review the article again to find out what information each article contains. Based on what you find you need to generate a list of questions that each article can answer.
