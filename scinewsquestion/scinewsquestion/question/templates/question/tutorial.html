Tutorial and Overview

In this session, you are going to read 3 news articles on a scientific research.



For each article, you are supposed to
  • (1) Read the article and generate some question on it.

    Read the article and raise some questions. Here each question can be about what you want to know/need while reading the article.

    You CAN generate duplicated question for different articles.

    You CAN see previous articles. However you SHOULD NOT add questions on the previous articles.

    • You can generate a question on a text range of your choice.


    • You can also generate a question on the right side-bar without specific text selection.


  • (2) Do the Post-Article survey

    Survey on the article itself and your experience.

  • (3) Push 'Next' button to proceed.
